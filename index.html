T4 Defense Tracker

How to use this tool

With the recent defense overhauls, hard caps are gone. For Torment 4 endgame content, aim for the practical diminishing-return caps to maximize your Toughness.

  1. Open your character's stat sheet in-game.
  2. Enter your current Total Armor.
  3. Enter your lowest Elemental Resistance percentage.
  4. Hit calculate to see how far you are from the T4 sweet spot (~13,350 Armor and 85% Resistances).
