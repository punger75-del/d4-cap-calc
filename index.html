Torment 4 Analyzer

Site Protocol:

1. Check your character sheet in Diablo 4.
2. Enter your total Armor and your lowest Resistance value below.
3. Hit 'Initiate Scan' to see how much gear you need to survive T4.

Trivial Fact: Since Season 4, Armor is hard-capped at 9,230. Having 10,000 armor gives the same benefit as 9,230.
Trivial Fact: Torment 4 hits you with a 100% resistance penalty. If you aren't capped at 70% after that penalty, you'll melt.
